## 5. Approaches to Maximize Sales with Video

Producing a stunning cinematic video is only the first step. To truly leverage video for maximum ROI and speed of sale, it must be part of a broader, strategic distribution plan. In 2026, how you promote the content is just as important as the content itself.

### Social Media Optimization: The "Reel" Strategy

Most buyers are scrolling through feeds on their phones. Your video needs to grab attention within the first three seconds.

- Vertical Formats: Ensure your video is produced in vertical (9:16) formats for Instagram Reels and TikTok. This ensures it fills the screen and feels native to the platform.
- Narrative Editing: Instead of a slow tour, use fast-paced editing set to trending audio to highlight the home’s best features (the view, the kitchen, the master ensuite).
- Captions and CTAs: Always include clear captions and a call-to-action (CTA) telling viewers to click the link in the bio for the full listing.

### Targeted Ad Campaigns: Reaching Qualified Buyers

Organic reach is good, but paid reach is precise. Using video in targeted ad campaigns allows you to find buyers actively searching for luxury homes.

- Geographic Targeting: Target ads to affluent areas outside of Vancouver or to international markets where buyers are looking to relocate.
- Behavioral Targeting: Target individuals interested in luxury brands, interior design, and real estate investment platforms.
- Retargeting: Show specific, high-end videos to people who have already visited your listing page, keeping the property top-of-mind.

### Complementary Technologies: The Immersive Experience

Videos work best when combined with other high-tech assets to create a complete digital experience.

- 3D Matterport Tours: While video tells the emotional story, a 3D tour allows the buyer to control their own experience, examining floor plans and room layouts in detail.
- Aerial Drone Production: Panoramic aerial shots provide context about the neighborhood, nearby parks, and ocean access, which are crucial for attracting out-of-town buyers.
